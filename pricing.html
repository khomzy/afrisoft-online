Understanding the Setup Fee

The one-time setup fee covers the initial heavy lifting: registering your domain, configuring your hosting, designing your website or app, setting up your emails, and training your team. Once paid, you only pay the retainer (monthly or yearly) for ongoing maintenance, hosting, and support.

Upgrades & Custom Requests

As your business grows, your needs will change. You can upgrade your package at any time. If you need custom features outside of your package, we will provide a clear, transparent quote before any extra work begins. No hidden fees, ever.
