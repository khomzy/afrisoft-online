Company Profile

Empowering Businesses with Complete Tech Solutions in Malawi.

Afrisoft Technologies is a proudly registered Malawian company specializing in both software and hardware products. Through our dedicated online branch, we provide comprehensive IT services to help businesses scale, automate, and succeed in the digital age.

Company image placeholder

Our Foundation: Hardware & Software

At our core, Afrisoft Technologies is a full-spectrum technology company. We don't just write code; we design and build physical hardware products alongside robust, custom software solutions. We understand the complete technology ecosystem because we build it from the ground up.

Our Online Branch: Business IT Services

To better serve the digital needs of modern enterprises, we launched our online branch. This dedicated division focuses exclusively on providing top-tier IT services, ensuring businesses have the reliable digital infrastructure, hosting, and automation they need to grow.

What We Build & Provide

From physical tech products to cloud-based services, we help businesses move from manual work to reliable digital systems.

  • Custom business software and operating systems
  • Hardware products and tech infrastructure
  • Business websites, domains, and cloud hosting
  • Mobile apps for customers and staff
  • AI agents for calls, messaging, and workflow support

Our Two Platforms

We operate with two clear public faces to serve different needs:

  • Afrisoft.store: Our hub for software products, hardware innovations, and tech experiments.
  • Afrisoft.online: Our dedicated branch for business IT services, hosting, mobile apps, and AI automation.
  • Local Expertise: Proudly Malawian, providing business-friendly, on-the-ground support.

Let's Build Something Great

Whether you need custom software, reliable hosting, or hardware solutions, our team in Malawi is ready to help your business grow. We partner with business owners to turn complex technical challenges into simple, scalable solutions.
