Cloud Hosting & Domains

Fast, Secure Hosting & Complete Domain Management.

Whether you need us to register a brand-new domain for your business, or you already own a domain from another provider, we provide reliable, high-speed hosting to keep your website live, secure, and backed up. We offer flexible monthly and yearly plans to suit your budget.

Hosting dashboard image placeholder

Hosting Domains You Already Own

Did you register your domain name somewhere else? No problem. We can seamlessly migrate your domain's DNS settings to our premium hosting servers. We handle all the technical configuration to ensure your website connects perfectly without any downtime.

Registering & Hosting New Domains

Starting from scratch? We will help you find and register the perfect domain name for your Malawian business. Once registered, we instantly set it up on our secure hosting environment, giving you a complete, ready-to-launch web presence from day one.

Reliability & Security

Your website needs to be online 24/7. We provide robust server infrastructure with high uptime guarantees. Every hosting package includes enterprise-grade security basics, SSL certificates to keep customer data safe, and proactive monitoring to stop threats before they happen.

Automated Backups & Maintenance

Never worry about losing your website data. We perform scheduled, automated backups of your website files and databases based on your specific package. We also handle routine server maintenance and updates, ensuring your site runs smoothly in the background.

Professional Business Email

Stop using generic @gmail.com addresses. We configure professional business email addresses (like info@yourbusiness.com) directly tied to your domain. This instantly boosts your credibility and makes your business look established and trustworthy to your clients.

Local Technical Support

When you need help, you don't have to deal with overseas chatbots. Because we are a Malawian company, you get direct, local technical support. If your website goes down or you need to add a new email account, our team is just a phone call away.

Flexible Monthly Plans

Perfect for startups and businesses that prefer to manage their cash flow closely. Our month-to-month hosting plans give you complete flexibility with no long-term lock-in. You get full access to our hosting, backups, and support, billed simply every month.

Discounted Yearly Plans

For established businesses looking for the best value, our yearly plans offer significant savings. By paying annually, you lock in a lower rate, eliminate monthly billing hassles, and ensure your domain and hosting are paid for and secure for the entire year.
